What is stress?

Stress is our body's response to pressure. Many different situations or life events can cause stress.
It is often triggered when we experience something new or unexpected that threatens our sense of self or
when we feel we have little control over a situation. We all deal with stress differently.

Emotional stress in the classroom happens due to a combination of factors emotionally mismanaged school like:

-lack of motivation

-Excessive charging

-Emotional disorders

-Tiredness

-Intrigues, school violence

-Fatigue

-Gossip

Personal dissatisfaction, generating anxiety and stress and low self-esteem.

Impact of Stress caused at School


- Tense school environment can affect your mental and physical health.

-Increased prevalence of psychological and physical problems. As depression, anxiety, nervousness
and stress-related disorders, which in turn may adversely affect the results academics.

-Loss of focus: The child will have more difficulty concentrating on other activities.

-Reduction in memorization capacity:

-Childhood stress can also influence the loss of memory capacity, as well as in the maintenance
and recovery of old memories. And of course, be being able to memorize information is essential for the development of any student, since he needs to
learn new things, remember what he has already studied, take tests and so on.

-From these impacts, children can face a series of problems within the school
environment. As:

-Difficulty understanding subjects;

-Difficulty concentrating during classes;

-Feeling of incapacity;

-Barriers to social interactions.


How to avoid Stress at School


-Stress affects students and teachers and can change the performance of both,
one of the ways to avoid stress It is:

- Have an organized study routine;

-Invest in physical activities;

-Avoid consuming stimulating foods;

-Sleep well;

-Taking care of emotional health

-Invest in motivational speeches for teachers, workshops and training focused on learning
new management techniques conflicts.

-Avoid gossip. This behavior is harmful and generates harm to those involved.

-Create projects on coexistence standards to incentivate the respect amongst people.

Mental health of students at Enko Maputo

Students at Enko Maputo feel that the school affects negatively their mental health.
One of the things that they say is the major cause of their school related stress is the timetabe.
They vocalised that the current timetable is not flexible and practical, rather they say that it is time-consuming and it puts pressure on them. Some solutions proposed by the students are as follow:

-Increase the breaktime;

-Change the timetable to more flexible hours;

-Leaving early foe IBDP students;

-Shortening the school time;

-Having more practical lessons.

We also looked at the school's point of view. They claim that they are already working on tryingto relieve some of the student's mental load.
They have proposed to provide a psicologist, shorten the schedule andgive longer breaks, but thise are just provisions of the school head.
By the words of the head of school "I would really love to give you guys a longer break, but it does not only depend on me...". The School decisions
are not only up to the Head of school but also to the whole board of school.
